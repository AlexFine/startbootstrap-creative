BASE Codes

BASE Codes, Bay Area Student Entrepreneurs and Coders, is a startup accelerator and venture fund for student entrepreneurs. We invest in student run companies and give them the network they need to succeed.

Find Out More

What is BASE?

BASE is a maker focused venture fund founded by students, for students. BASE helps it's companies in two ways, through mentorship and an initial seed round. When you become a BASE company we become a part of your team. We give you the developers and tools you need to make and develop your product. We also provide a small seed round to help jump start your company.
